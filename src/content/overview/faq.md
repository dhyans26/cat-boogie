## General

<details>
<summary>What is Project YSWS?</summary>

Project YSWS lets Hack Club leaders create their own "You Ship, We Ship" workshop. You design a beginner-friendly workshop, run it at your club, and Hack Club funds it at up to $5 USD per hour per participant.

</details>

<details>
<summary>Who can apply?</summary>

Any active Hack Club leader can apply. You need to be running a club and have a clear idea for a workshop you'd like to build.

</details>

<details>
<summary>How much funding can I get?</summary>

Hack Club funds workshops at up to $5 USD per hour per participant. The exact amount depends on your workshop plan and scope.

</details>

## Getting Started

<details>
<summary>How do I apply?</summary>

Read through the overview section to understand the process, then fill out the application form on the [Apply](/overview/apply) page. Make sure you've read the guide on building good workshops before applying.

</details>

<details>
<summary>What makes a good workshop?</summary>

Good workshops are beginner-friendly, project-based, and have clear outcomes. Participants should ship something tangible by the end. Check out the [Guide](/overview/guide) for more detail on what we're looking for.

</details>

<details>
<summary>How long does the review process take?</summary>

Review times can vary, but you'll typically hear back within a couple of weeks. The mentors review each application carefully to give useful feedback.

</details>

## Running Your Workshop

<details>
<summary>Can I scale my workshop to other clubs?</summary>

Yes! If your workshop runs successfully at your own club, Hack Club may invite you to expand it into an official YSWS that other clubs worldwide can run.

</details>

<details>
<summary>What if I need help along the way?</summary>

All Project YSWS mentors have been in your shoes before and are happy to help. Don't hesitate to reach out in the Hack Club Slack for guidance or advice.

</details>
